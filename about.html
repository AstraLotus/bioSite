Evan's Hobbies: A Blend of Enjoyment and Thrills

Evan Smith is a talented and enthusiastic 29-year-old rigger at the Moody Center in Austin, Texas. Born and raised in Grand Prairie, Evan has had a passion for theater and film since childhood. This passion led him to study film at the University of North Texas, where he honed his skills and learned about the industry.

Evan has always been drawn to the fast-paced, creative environment of live performance. As a rigger at the Moody Center, he’s been able to bring his passion to life by working on concerts, stand-up shows, and other live events. Evan takes pride in his work, knowing that his rigging helps bring these productions to life.

In addition to his rigging work, Evan has been able to expand his skill set by getting involved in other areas of production, such as camera operation and lighting. This has allowed him to gain a well-rounded understanding of the industry and make important connections with his colleagues.

When asked about his experience working at the Moody Center, Evan stated, “Working at the Moody Center has been a dream come true for me. I feel lucky to be a part of such a talented and supportive team, and I love the impact I have on each production.”

When he’s not working, Evan loves to explore Austin and create content as a filmmaker. With his passion, drive, and skills, Evan is poised for a bright future in the entertainment industry.
